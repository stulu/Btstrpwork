Banana bread

Below you will find the ingredients and other stuff needed in order for you to bake this delicious healthy cake.


Quantity Products
225g Self-raising flour
130g Muscovado sugar
110ml Oil
3 Overripe bananas
1 teaspoon Baking powder
1 teaspoon Vanilla essence
1/2 teaspoon  Ginger powder

Method and other information


  • Mix dry and wet ingrediants separately then combine
  • Pour into baking tin or mold
  • Bake time 45 min on a low to medium heat
  • Allow to cool on a rack
  • Serves 8
